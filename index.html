Who We Are

Dedicated to Children's Growth & Development

Tip Top Consultancy is an educational products and services company operating across Rwanda and Uganda. We are passionate about empowering children with tools they need to thrive emotionally, intellectually, and socially.

Our child-friendly workbooks, readers, toys, and consultancy services align with African ECD and pre-school national curricula — delivering unparalleled learning experiences to children and their families.

What We Offer

Our Products & Services

Everything a child needs to learn, grow, and flourish — designed by education experts and loved by children across Africa.

📗

Educational Books

Our workbooks and readers align with ECD and pre-school syllabi, covering health activities, social skills, numeracy, and cognitive development. Designed to make reading fun and effective.

🧸

Learning Toys

Research-backed toys that make learning through play natural. Our toys stimulate curiosity, reduce school dropout rates, and help children discover who they are through healthy play.

🗂️

Charts & Flashcards

Colorful, visually engaging wall charts and flashcards that help children memorize vocabulary effortlessly. Perfect for classroom and home use alike.

🏫

Consultancy Services

Expert training and custom manuals for caregivers and parents on positive parenting, child protection, social inclusion, and gender-sensitive education.

Consultancy Services

Our consultancy program enriches the "Learn for Change" initiative by empowering the adults in a child's life — parents, caregivers, and teachers alike.

  • Positive parenting training and interactive workshops
  • Child protection frameworks and community awareness
  • Social inclusion and diversity-sensitive education
  • Gender-sensitive curriculum guidance for educators
  • Custom manuals and reference materials for schools
  • Institutional capacity building for NGOs and schools
